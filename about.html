GDG Ghana

Google Developer Group (GDG) Ghana is a vibrant community of Ghanaian technologists and enthusiasts that actively employ Google technologies for work and pleasure.We believe that technology is an important key to development in the country and in Africa, and our focus is building local capacity to make that happen.We play a lead role in the Vim Series, a weekly Thursday technology talk series held in Accra.

Disclaimer: GDG Ghana is an independent group; our activities and the opinions expressed here should in no way be linked to Google, the corporation.

Meet the Team

A group of volunteering individuals who are passionate about Google technologies, passionate enough to organise events for the others who are interested in Google Tech
